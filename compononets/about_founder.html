Our story

Fluffier Dreams for Abuja ☁️

Stackit started with a simple obsession: why should great desserts only live in restaurants? Vicky brought the first mobile mini pancake cart to the heart of Nigeria, blending luxury catering with a playful street-food soul. Every stack is made fresh, every topping is premium, and every event is treated like a main stage performance. We don't just serve food; we serve memories.
